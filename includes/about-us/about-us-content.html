Telecommunication has a significant social, cultural and economic impact on modern society Telecommunication has played a significant role in social relationships.Nevertheless, devices like the telephone system were originally advertised with an emphasis on the practical dimensions of the device (such as the ability to conduct business or order home services) as opposed to the social dimensions. Therefore, the social dimensions of the device became a prominent theme in telephone advertisements. new promotions started appealing to consumers emotions, stressing the importance of social conversations and staying connected to family and friends.

Global Business

Startelecom is a subsidiary of the APEX Japan Travel Group, an international destination management company specializing in inbound tour services within Asia. Established since 1975, APEX Japan Travel Group have been providing professional land tour service and arrangements all over Asia and is still continuously working with various local and foreign based travel companies that create itineraries for new destinations. Startelecom is opening its doors to new potential markets in its effort to expand its services from Australia and New Zealand to new Japanese clients. Developing mobile pocket WIFI as one of the convenience and tour benefits to travelers, no matter from Japan, Australia or Vietnam to other nations or in vice versa.
